Frequently Asked Question

We’ve answered the most common questions to help you get started with confidence.

Do I keep full rights to my book?
Absolutely. You keep 100% ownership of your work. We simply help you prepare, design, and publish it — all rights stay with you.
How much does publishing a book cost?
It depends on what stage your book is in. Some authors only need formatting, while others need editing, cover design, and distribution. We’ll share a clear quote before anything starts no surprise fees.
Can you help with book cover design too?
Yes! We have an in-house design team that creates both digital and print covers. Whether you want something minimal or bold, we make sure it fits your story.
Do you offer editing or proofreading?
Of course. We offer light editing, full line editing, and proofreading depending on your needs. Every manuscript deserves polish before it hits the shelf.
Will my book be available on Amazon or bookstores?

Yes! We publish across multiple platforms including Amazon (Kindle + Print), Barnes & Noble, Apple Books, and more  depending on your chosen plan.

What if I’ve never published before?

No problem at all. Most of our authors are first-timers! We walk you through each step from manuscript prep to live listings — with full support.

How long does the publishing process take?
Typically between 3 to 8 weeks, depending on editing and design revisions. We don’t rush quality, but we also don’t keep you waiting forever.
Can I use my own ISBN?
Yes, you can bring your own or we can assign one for you. Either way, the ISBN stays linked to your author name not ours.
Do you help with book marketing or promotions?
Yes! We offer optional add-ons like author website design, book trailers, social media campaigns, and press release writing to help your book reach readers.
What file format do I need to send my manuscript in?
A Word document (.docx) or Google Docs file is perfect. We’ll handle formatting, typesetting, and converting it to print and eBook-ready files.
Is this self-publishing or traditional publishing?
We specialize in independent publishing support meaning you keep all rights and royalties, and we handle the technical and creative work.
Can you re-publish an already printed book?
Yes, we can reformat, redesign, and re-launch your existing book — even if it was published elsewhere. Many authors come to us for better visibility and design upgrades.
